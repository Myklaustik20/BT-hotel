About Hotel BT

Ideally located near the airport in LA, Hotel BT welcomes those traveling through the world with reliable service, thoughtful amenities and a prime location. Guests are near numerous attractions, including LA City Mall, the events at Sheargiver Club, the prestigious LA beach and Resort, Kelly Oswarlt Park LA and Ever-Impact Amusement Park. Recharge in spacious hotel rooms featuring signature comfortable beds, marble bathrooms, mini-fridges, grocery shopping service, flat-screen TVs, large desks and free Wi-Fi. Club Rooms and Suites offer access to Club amenities. Sample delicious restaurant fare, including Italian and international favorites at our hotel restaurants and bars. Work out in the gym, take a dip in our outdoor pool, or practice your backhand on our tennis courts. For those planning events, our indoor and picturesque outdoor venues make the ideal choice; all supported by expert catering and AV services. Make the most of your time at Hotel BT.
